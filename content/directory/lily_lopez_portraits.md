My name is Lily, wife to a loving husband and father.  I’m also a mother and educator to my two beautiful girls.  I have always been very crafty, both in art and sewing, and being that we are a homeschool family, arts and crafts are a large part of our learning environment.

However, my passion is photography as an art form.  I have been a professional photographer for 10 years.  With plenty of formal training and hands on experience, I’m always looking for the right light for that perfect shot.  Every detail in a portrait is analyzed: your pose, your body language, the background, the colors, and so much more.  With today’s technology, we all take pictures for granted, but a portrait that is crafted with an artful eye is one that can display in your home and cherish for a lifetime.
